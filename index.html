About Sal United Company

Our Objective: At Sal United Company, our prospective is to maintain the quality of service in every aspect of the work to the client. We are competent in the market with our strategy of delivering in time and cost-effectively, ensuring client satisfaction and project success.

We are a dedicated team of qualified, trained, and well-experienced Engineers and Technicians committed to providing 24/7 support. Our expertise spans comprehensive mechanical and electrical solutions, specialized marine services, and robust construction capabilities.

With over 35 years of experience in various industrial and marine projects, including significant infrastructure and maintenance works, we pride ourselves on our ability to handle complex challenges and deliver superior results for our clients across Saudi Arabia and Oman.
